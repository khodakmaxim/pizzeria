Delivery and payment

Delivery

We work for you every day from 10:00 to 22:00

Delivery is made to the address that you can specify when placing an order

You also have the opportunity to pick up your order with a pleasant 10% discount at the address of the city of Vyshneve, 42 Evropeyska Street

(in case of self-delivery, a 10% discount is provided only if the order is placed by phone number or through the website, selecting "self-delivery (Vyshneve, 42 Evropeyska Street)")

List of settlements available for delivery by our courier:

• Vyshneve

• Sofiivska Borshchahivka

• Kryukivshchyna

• Gatne

• Sviatopetrivske

Delivery takes place within 45 min. Depending on the volume of the order and the busyness of the establishment, the delivery time may be increased

Payment

Payment for the order is possible in any convenient way for you:

• cash or courier card, when ordering through the website or by phone

• cash or by card upon self-delivery

• prepayment by card, when ordering through the website

Delivery cost - 50 uah
